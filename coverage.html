What's Covered in Detail

All three of our carrier partners (Lemonade, ASPCA, and Spot) offer comprehensive coverage with these core protections:

🤕

Accidents & Injuries

Coverage for unexpected accidents and injuries that require veterinary treatment.

Broken Bones & Fractures

Coverage for fractures from falls, accidents, or trauma requiring casting, surgery, or splinting.

Lacerations & Wounds

Treatment for cuts, punctures, and wounds including stitches, antibiotics, and follow-up care.

Foreign Object Ingestion

Coverage for pets who swallow toys, bones, or other objects requiring endoscopic removal or surgery.

Poisoning & Toxins

Emergency treatment for ingestion of toxic substances including induced vomiting, antidotes, and hospitalization.

Burns & Trauma

Treatment for thermal, chemical, or electrical burns and other traumatic injuries.

Dental Injuries

Accidental damage to teeth or mouth from trauma, not related to dental disease.

🦠

Illnesses & Infections

Coverage for a wide range of illnesses and infections that affect your pet's health.

Skin Conditions

Treatment for dermatitis, hot spots, allergic skin reactions, and bacterial/fungal infections.

Ear & Eye Infections

Coverage for otitis, conjunctivitis, and other infections requiring medication and treatment.

Urinary Tract Infections (UTIs)

Diagnosis and treatment of bladder and urinary tract infections including testing and antibiotics.

Respiratory Infections

Coverage for kennel cough, pneumonia, and other respiratory illnesses.

Gastrointestinal Issues

Treatment for vomiting, diarrhea, pancreatitis, and other digestive system illnesses.

Parasitic Infections

Coverage for serious parasitic infections like heartworm disease, giardia, and tick-borne illnesses.

🔬

Diagnostics & Lab Tests

Essential diagnostic testing to accurately diagnose your pet's condition.

Blood Panels & Chemistry

Complete blood count (CBC), chemistry panels, and specialized blood tests to assess organ function.

Urinalysis

Laboratory analysis of urine to detect infections, kidney issues, and other health problems.

Imaging: X-Rays & Ultrasounds

Diagnostic imaging including radiographs and ultrasounds to visualize internal structures.

Advanced Imaging

Coverage for MRIs, CT scans, and other advanced imaging when medically necessary.

Cytology & Biopsies

Cell analysis and tissue sampling for cancer diagnosis and other conditions.

Fecal Exams & Parasite Testing

Testing for intestinal parasites, bacterial infections, and other GI issues.

🏥

Surgery & Hospitalization

Coverage for surgical procedures and hospital stays when your pet needs intensive care.

Emergency Surgery

Life-saving surgical procedures for acute conditions like bloat, intestinal obstruction, or trauma.

Planned Elective Surgery

Coverage for necessary surgeries like ACL repair, tumor removal, and orthopedic procedures.

Overnight Hospitalization

Costs for ICU care, monitoring, and nursing care during hospital stays.

Post-Operative Care

Follow-up visits, wound care, medication, and rehabilitation after surgery.

Anesthesia & Monitoring

Costs for anesthesia administration and vital sign monitoring during procedures.

Specialist surgeries

Coverage for procedures performed by veterinary specialists (orthopedic, neurologist, etc.).

💊

Prescription Medications

Coverage for prescription medications prescribed by your veterinarian.

Antibiotics

Full coverage for prescribed antibiotic medications to treat bacterial infections.

Pain Management

Prescription pain medications including NSAIDs, opioids, and other analgesics.

Anti-Inflammatories

Steroidal and non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs for inflammation and swelling.

Chronic Disease Meds

Long-term medications for conditions like diabetes, hypothyroidism, and heart disease.

Behavioral Medications

Prescription drugs for anxiety, aggression, or compulsive behaviors (varies by carrier).

Specialty Drugs

Coverage for expensive specialty medications including chemotherapy drugs and biologics.

🧬

Hereditary & Congenital Conditions

Coverage for breed-specific and genetic conditions that may appear later in life.

Hip Dysplasia

Diagnosis, treatment, and surgery for hip dysplasia common in large breed dogs.

Heart Conditions

Coverage for congenital heart defects and hereditary heart diseases like murmurs and valve issues.

Eye Disorders

Treatment for progressive retinal atrophy, cataracts, and other hereditary eye conditions.

Patellar Luxation

Surgical and medical treatment for dislocated kneecaps common in small breeds.

Hernias

Coverage for umbilical, inguinal, and diaphragmatic hernias present at birth.

Breed-Specific Conditions

Coverage for conditions common in specific breeds (e.g., German Shepherds, Bulldogs, Persians).

🦷

Dental Illness

Coverage for dental diseases and conditions that affect oral health.

Tooth Extractions

Coverage for extracting broken, infected, or diseased teeth under anesthesia.

Gingivitis & Periodontal Disease

Treatment for gum disease including deep cleaning, antibiotics, and surgical intervention.

Oral Infections & Abscesses

Coverage for bacterial infections, tooth abscesses, and related treatments.

Mouth Tumors & Growths

Diagnosis and removal of oral masses, cysts, and tumors (benign or malignant).

Jaw & Facial Bone Issues

Coverage for fractures, infections, or abnormalities of the jaw and facial bones.

Stomatitis

Severe inflammation of the mouth and gums requiring extensive treatment.

🎗️

Cancer Treatment

Comprehensive coverage for cancer diagnosis and treatment.

Chemotherapy

Multiple chemotherapy sessions, anti-nausea medications, and supportive care during treatment.

Radiation Therapy

Coverage for radiation treatment sessions including planning and delivery.

Cancer Surgery

Major surgical procedures to remove tumors, including complex reconstructive surgery.

Diagnostic Testing

Biopsies, imaging (CT/MRI), blood work, and staging tests for cancer diagnosis.

Hospitalization & ICU

Extended hospital stays for intensive care during cancer treatment.

Palliative Care

Pain management and quality-of-life care for pets with terminal cancer.

🚑

Emergency & Specialty Care

Coverage for emergency visits and specialist veterinary care.

24/7 Emergency Care

After-hours emergency clinic visits, triage, and emergency treatments.

Specialist Referrals

Visits to veterinary specialists including neurologists, cardiologists, oncologists, etc.

Rehabilitation Therapy

Physical therapy, hydrotherapy, acupuncture, and rehabilitation for recovery.

Critical Care

Intensive care unit (ICU) monitoring and treatment for life-threatening conditions.

Oxygen Therapy

Emergency oxygen administration and respiratory support.

Blood Transfusions

Emergency blood transfusions and related laboratory testing.

🌡️

Chronic Conditions

Ongoing coverage for chronic illnesses that require long-term management.

Diabetes Management

Insulin, glucose monitors, syringes, and regular check-ups for diabetic pets.

Arthritis & Joint Disease

Pain management, joint supplements, anti-inflammatories, and physical therapy.

Allergies

Diagnosis, treatment, and management of environmental and food allergies.

Hypothyroidism

Hormone replacement therapy and regular blood monitoring for thyroid conditions.

Epilepsy & Seizures

Anti-seizure medications, emergency treatment for breakthrough seizures, and monitoring.

Kidney Disease

Medication, special diets, and regular monitoring for chronic kidney disease.
